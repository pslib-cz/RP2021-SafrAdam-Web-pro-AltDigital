About Us

The alt digital company has been focusing on electronics development, programming and production of printed circuit boards since 2018. We are a member of the Jablotron group. Jablotron is one of the largest manufacturers of electronic systems in the Czech Republic, which exports its products to more than 80 countries around the world. This helps us to guarantee high demands on quality in production.

These basic pillars opened up market opportunities for us and give us the opportunity to offer product development as a comprehensive service from feasibility study through design to production itself. In cooperation with production companies in the holding, such as JabloPCB, we are able to guarantee quality and coordinate large-scale production.

Based on our experience, quality team, wide background and also scientific history of founding members, we are happy to accept challenges on the verge of technical feasibility.
